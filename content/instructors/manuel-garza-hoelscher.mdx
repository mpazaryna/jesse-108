Manuel Garza Hoelscher is a dedicated yoga teacher whose classes are rooted in presence, strength, and mindful awareness. With a calming energy and a deep respect for the practice, Manuel creates space for students to explore movement as a path to inner stillness and self-discovery. His teaching is both grounding and uplifting, offering a powerful connection between breath, body, and soul.
