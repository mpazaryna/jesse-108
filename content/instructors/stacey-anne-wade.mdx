Stacey Anne Wade is a passionate and heart-led yoga teacher who brings authenticity, presence, and soul to her classes. With a deep commitment to personal growth and collective healing, Stacey guides students through practices that nurture the body, quiet the mind, and awaken the spirit. Her classes are an invitation to show up fully—just as you are—with breath, movement, and compassion.
