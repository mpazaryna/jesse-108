Elena Slade is a passionate and intuitive yoga teacher and Master Reiki Healer, who brings warmth, healing, and grace to every class. Blending mindful movement with subtle energy work, she creates nurturing spaces for students to reconnect with themselves on all levels—body, mind, and spirit. Elena’s teaching invites presence, inner balance, and a deep sense of peace both on and off the mat.
